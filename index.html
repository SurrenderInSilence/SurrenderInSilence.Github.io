About the Band

Beauty and the storm, in one voice.

Surrender in Silence is a symphonic metalcore band out of Staten Island, NYC, that combines powerful guitar riffs with dramatic orchestral arrangements, blending the two for a dynamic sound.

Drawing inspiration from bands such as Epica, Nightwish, Eleine, Blackbriar, and Evanescence, Surrender In Silence is set to take their place in the music scene.
